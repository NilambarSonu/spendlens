# SpendLens — Product Unit Economics & Lead Valuations

This document presents a granular financial model showing how SpendLens acts as a high-ROI, low-CAC acquisition channel, converting anonymous audits into high-value B2B customers for Credex's core credit products.

---

## 1. The Marketing Loop & Acquisition Unit Economics

As a free tool with zero sign-up friction, SpendLens minimizes CAC compared to traditional performance marketing.

| Financial Dimension | Estimated Value | Explanatory Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fully Loaded CAC** | **$4.50** | Based on developer hosting costs ($15/mo serverless), Claude API tokens ($0.02/audit), and organic/PLG content distributions. |
| **Audit to Lead Conversion** | **18%** | Percentage of visitors completing audits who input their emails inside our Value-First Lead capture. |
| **Lead to Sales Call MQL** | **5%** | Percentage of captured leads exceeding the $500/mo overspend threshold who book a free consultation. |
| **Qualified Call to Customer**| **25%** | Close rate of high-savings leads transitioning to Credex's custom discounted bulk credit programs. |
| **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**| **$8,400** | Calculated on a 2% commission of $350,000 yearly average enterprise AI spend (retained for 12 months). |

## 3. Financial Plan: Path to $1M ARR for Credex

To reach **$1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue** (roughly $83,333 in monthly commission net revenue), Credex must scale its B2B volume discounting model through SpendLens lead captures:

### Target Matrix:
- **Core Commission Percentage**: 2.5% of total audited startup spend routed to Credex bulk channels.
- **Average Startup AI Spend**: $150,000/year (Cursor Pro + ChatGPT Team + Heavy direct API consumption for production).
- **Average Commission Revenue per Account**: $150,000 * 2.5% = **$3,750/year** (or $312/mo).
- **Target Active Accounts**: **267 Startup Customers**.

### SpendLens Funnel Scale Requirements:
- At a 0.9% visitor-to-deal conversion rate:
  - Total Visitors Required: **29,666 unique visitors**.
  - Weekly traffic: ~600 unique visitors (easily achievable through continuous viral launches and HN positioning).
- By providing direct, un-hallucinated value, SpendLens turns a traditional corporate sales process into a self-serve diagnostic utility, cutting sales cycles from 90 days to under 48 hours.
